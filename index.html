Frequently asked questions

Not sure how DPA can help you? Wonder why you need to subscribe?

What is the initial start up process like?

We try to make the DPA start-up process as smooth as possible. Once your interest has been confirmed, you should receive a proposal from an assigned sales staff. A data collection form will be sent out to your school after payment has been made. Then the information collected from this will be used to configure your school’s DPA database.
After this, a staff training will now be scheduled which usually takes 3 days or less (depending on the number of your staff that we need to train). Once the training process is complete, your school can begin using DPA smoothly.

Can I adopt DPA in the middle of a school session?

We usually advice starting with DPA at the beginning of a new session, to allow enrollment officers and admin a chance to start entering records from a fresh academic year. However, a good number of schools have successfully introduced DPA during the second or even third school term. It all depends on you!

Is it true I can start using DPA for free?

You can use DPA for free for one (1) term on a trial basis, after which you will be required to pay for a subscription plan to continue using the system. Every school is entitled to ONLY 1 term free trial.

How secure is my data on DPA?

Safety of your data stored on DPA is 100%, guaranteed. With decentralized log-in access for every staff role, each staff can access information that only concerns their job role.
In addition, all of DPA information is hosted on a very secure cloud service which uses network architecture built to meet the requirements of the most security-sensitive organizations.
